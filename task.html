The ingredients we need for a juicy burger

Ingredients Amount
Beef 600g
Burger bread 6pcs
Burger mix 5 teaspoons
Sugar 1 teaspoon
Lettuce 200g
Cheese 12 slices
Pickles Atleast 20 pcs
Butter 2 teaspoons
Mayo 2 teaspoons
Ketchup 2 teaspoons

Instructions

  1. Mix beef with spices
  2. Butter the pan and toast bread
  3. Prep lettuce
  4. Prep pickles
  5. Mix sauce ratio 1 ketchup per 3 mayos
  6. Preheat the pan with butter
  7. Grill burgers for 2 minutes per side
  8. Seasone as much as you like
  9. Take the bottom of the burger and add sauce
  10. Add lettuce and bruger
  11. On top of burger add cheese
  12. Top it of with some pickels and but on her hat
  13. Enjoy the juicy burger!
